Hungry, Humble, Honest, with Heart. The OpportunityAre you a passionate and innovative student with a strong foundation in programming and a desire to dive deep into Software-Defined Networking (SDN) solutions? If so, joining our team as a Software Engineer Intern at Nutanix offers you a unique opportunity to enhance your technical skills through hands-on projects, collaborate with industry experts, and contribute to cutting-edge networking services that shape the future of cloud computing. About the TeamSoftware-defined Network Engineering is responsible for designing, developing, and implementing end-to-end services, including software-defined network (SDN) solutions, with a specific focus on cloud, distributed systems, scale, and performance. Your RoleDesign and create documentation for multi-tenant network solutions within the Nutanix Cloud Platform.Collaborate with technical staff and mentors to define project scopes and critical results.Develop, implement, and test end-to-end networking services leveraging Software-Defined Networking (SDN).Strengthen programming skills through hands-on technical projects using Python, Java, and/or Golang.Engage in ongoing training and mentorship from industry experts to gain insights into software-defined solutions.Contribute meaningfully to the team’s mission and deliver improved networking services for hybrid and multi-cloud environments.Present project findings and personal development learnings at the conclusion of the internship.Integrate into the team swiftly by communicating consistently and collaborating with peers and leadership. What You Will BringApplicants must be authorized to work in the US without requiring current or future sponsorship for an employment visa, such as an F-1, H-1 B, or TN visa.Strong programming skills in Python, Java, and/or Golang.Solid understanding of Linux operating systems and networking concepts.Knowledge of software-defined networking (SDN) principles and multi-tenant architectures.Exposure to cloud computing and hybrid cloud environments.Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams and technical staff.Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn through hands-on experience.Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.Effective communication skills for articulating technical concepts and project progress.Perks You'll Enjoy A structured Campus to Career program with training and mentorship, a hackathon, leadership roundtable discussions, and the chance to showcase your achievements at the close of your internshipPlentiful opportunities for learning, collaboration, and connection in personA detailed look inside our technical Nutanix platform architectureCompetitive pay, Wellness Days, and a vibrant Employee Resource Group communityThe Process We understand you are pursuing multiple employment options. Please note these important steps. Application Screening: We will review your application to understand your unique talents and skills in relation to our opportunities Online Technical Assessment: We will review your foundational skills through a coding skills assessment in the language of your choiceTechnical interviews with our hiring teams to showcase your skills Students who advance to this round will receive preparation prior to the interviews Expect up to 3 interviews with Nutanix engineers and leaders. These are designed to assess your technical skills, role readiness, and your motivation for the opportunity at Nutanix. Each meeting will offer you an opportunity to ask questions and learn more about the team you’ll potentially work with Final Decision: Offers extended to finalists within 2 days of the technical interview Work ArrangementHybrid: This role operates in a hybrid capacity, blending the benefits of remote work with the advantages of in-person collaboration. In locations where our workplace policy applies (i.e., San Jose, Durham, Mexico City, Bangalore, Pune, Hoofddorp, Belgrade, Barcelona, Singapore, Sydney, and Tokyo), employees are expected to work onsite a minimum of 3 days per week to foster collaboration, team alignment, and access to in-office resources. Workplace type may vary based on location and team requirements. Please speak with your recruiter for details. Additional team-specific guidance and norms will be provided by your manager. var lastScrollTop = 0;
$(window).scroll(function (event) {
var st = $(this).scrollTop();
if (st > 0) {
if (st > lastScrollTop) {
// downscroll code
$('header').addClass('nav-down');
$('header').addClass('smaller')
} else {
// uproll code
$('header').removeClass('smaller');
$('header').removeClass('nav-down');
$('header').addClass('nav-up')
}
} else {
$('header').removeClass('nav-up');
$('header').removeClass('smaller');
}
lastScrollTop = st;
});
jQuery(document).ready(function() {
var offset = 250;
var duration = 300;
jQuery(window).scroll(function() {
if (jQuery(this).scrollTop() > offset) {
jQuery('.back-to-top').fadeIn(duration);
} else {
jQuery('.back-to-top').fadeOut(duration);
}
});
jQuery('.back-to-top').click(function(event) {
event.preventDefault();
jQuery('html, body').animate({scrollTop: 0}, duration);
return false;
})
});
