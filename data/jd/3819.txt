We are actively seeking an undergraduate 2026 LG Summer Intern to join our DXP Architecture & AI Team.
Our AI Internship is a 9-week program from June 1st through July 31st .
The internship will be based in the office (Mon-Fri 8AM-5PM) at the LG North America Innovation Campus (HQ) in Englewood Cliffs, NJ and pay an hourly rate of $25.00.
The LG Internship Program is built on the philosophy of ensuring that each intern is given the opportunity to perform robust and meaningful work while gaining real-world experience.
The program will also include each intern working on an individual strategic project for the department and delivering a presentation at the program’s end.
The main project for the AI Intern is:
- Support the development of AI-driven tools and prototypes designed to improve internal productivity, knowledge accessibility, and business process automation.
- Assisting in the development of internal generative AI applications, building automation workflows using AI models, and evaluating machine learning solutions that can support business operations.
- Contribute to the exploration and development of AI capabilities that can be integrated into future products, internal tools and workflows.
- Gain hands on experience with cutting edge AI technologies while delivering meaningful insights and prototypes to support LG's AI strategy
Responsibilities
To support the DXP AI Team in developing, testing and deploying AI-driven solutions that improve operational efficiency and enable data-driven decision making across the enterprise
Work closely with engineers, product managers, and business stakeholders to assist in the development of machine learning and generative AI applications
Assist in the development and testing of AI models, including large language model (LLM) based applications
Support data preparation, cleaning and feature engineering for machine learning workflows
Conduct research on emerging AI technologies and identifying potential use cases relevant to business needs
Qualifications
Enrollment in a Bachelor's degree program at an accredited College/University.
Rising Sophomore, Rising Junior in a business, data, or analytics related major is preferred
Programing experience with Python preferred
Prior exposure to LLMs or generative AI technologies preferred
Familiarity with GiT or version control systems
Understanding of APIs and basic software development practices
Basic skills in quantitative data analysis and be proficient in using Excel for creating charts and infographics
Proficiency in tools such as Microsoft Office, Google, Teams, and AI-based drawing tools is required for visualizing ideas
Demonstrates a strong interest in diverse generations and cultures, particularly related to food lifestyles in both the U.S. and Korea
Curiosity and enthusiasm for customer observation, the ability to extract meaningful insights, and strong communication skills are essential qualities
Mindset grounded in design thinking and the ability to approach and solve problems creatively
Strong communication skills, outgoing personality
