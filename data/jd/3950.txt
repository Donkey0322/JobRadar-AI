Welcome to 247Sports, the industry leader in recruiting content with a network of team sites devoted to college sports. 247Sports delivers free and premium content and online communities for the most dedicated and engaged college sports fans. The 247Sports engineering team manages the backend and frontend applications that power both the editorial experience for internal staff and college partners, and the customer experience for our users of the platform.
* Collaborate with product and business leaders to transform their creative vision into code.* Work with engineering experts to execute on needle-moving feature development and improve the customer experience through addressing functional feedback.* Migrate legacy application flows into modern, maintainable frameworks.* Participate in agile ceremonies and engineering team communications.
* Computer Science or related major* Knowledge of Git Version Control* Familiarity with interacting with REST APIs* Preferable to have experience with Java, .NET, NodeJS, React, ExpressJS, or similar modern frontend technology frameworks.
Eligibility:
This internship is available to undergraduate students who will be Juniors or Seniors in the fall of 2026. If you are a graduating Senior, please check our website for full-time job opportunities.
You must be available to intern full-time (5 days a week/36-40 hours a week) for 10 weeks total starting in June.
Interns will be required to work fully on-site for the duration of the program. Note: Paramount does not provide financial relocation support or housing stipends.
Internships are paid at a competitive standard hourly rate (school credit is optional).
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States on a full‑time basis.
Employment eligibility to work with Paramount in the United States is required as the company does not engage in sponsorship for internships.
