Job Summary:
About the Role & Program The ABC News Product Management and Engineering teams are responsible for designing, maintaining, building, and acquiring the technology solutions and optimizing workflows that enable ABC News to create and deliver the news every single day. In this internship, you will be working with product managers and engineers helping to assess, plan and test new workflows, software and system deployments, develop training materials, and troubleshoot issues as they come up. Every day there will be a new opportunity to learn something about what it takes to be the #1 News organization in the country!The approximate dates of this internship are June 2026 through early January 2027.What You Will DoAs our intern, you will be rolling up your sleeves both metaphorically and literally to get to the bottom of all problems faced. On some days, you will spend time with our editorial, newsgathering, and operations teams to understand their workflows and then meet with engineers to propose and implement timesaving solutions. Then on the next day, you might work with engineers to put together an equipment list for a new studio or production build and ensure delivery will meet our needs.Required Qualifications & Skills About ABC News:
The Disney Entertainment Television group creates original entertainment and news content for the Company’s streaming platforms and its cable and broadcast networks.
About The Walt Disney Company:
The Walt Disney Company, together with its subsidiaries and affiliates, is a leading diversified international family entertainment and media enterprise that includes three core business segments: Disney Entertainment, ESPN, and Disney Experiences. From humble beginnings as a cartoon studio in the 1920s to its preeminent name in the entertainment industry today, Disney proudly continues its legacy of creating world-class stories and experiences for every member of the family. Disney’s stories, characters and experiences reach consumers and guests from every corner of the globe. With operations in more than 40 countries, our employees and cast members work together to create entertainment experiences that are both universally and locally cherished.
This position is with American Broadcasting Companies, Inc., which is part of a business we call ABC News.
