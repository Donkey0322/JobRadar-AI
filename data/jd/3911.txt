Who We Are
Impiricus is the first and only AI-powered HCP Engagement Engine. In 2025, Deloitte named Impiricus the #1 fastest growing company in North America for their prestigious Fast 500 list. Founded by a practicing physician and a senior pharmaceutical executive, Impiricus was created to transform how life sciences companies support physicians. We ethically connect HCPs to pharma resources, reduce go-to-market costs and accelerate patient access to the treatments they need.
With our unique access to the largest opted-in network of HCPs, their insights, and clinical expertise, we are the leading provider of AI technology and real-time channels that life science companies need to deliver clear, reliable, and evidence-based resources directly into the hands of HCPs. Guided by a council of 2000+ trusted HCP advisors, we ensure every interaction is clinically meaningful, ethically grounded and leads to better patient care.
Job Summary
As a Software Engineer (Full-stack) Intern at Impiricus, you will contribute to building and improving digital healthcare solutions that support clinicians and healthcare organizations. You will work alongside experienced engineers to develop features across backend services and frontend applications, gaining hands-on experience with modern software development practices.
This internship is an opportunity to learn how scalable, production systems are designed and built in a fast-moving healthcare technology environment. You will collaborate with engineering, product, and design teams while developing practical skills in full-stack development, cloud infrastructure, and emerging AI technologies.
Responsibilities
Assist in developing and maintaining backend services and APIs primarily using Python within a microservices-based architecture.
Contribute to building and improving web applications and dashboards using modern frontend frameworks such as React
Help integrate AI and LLM-powered features into product workflows, including experimentation with prompts and backend integrations.
Write clean, well-documented, and tested code while learning engineering best practices including code reviews and CI/CD workflows.
Support the integration of APIs, databases, and internal services to enable efficient data exchange across platforms.
Collaborate with product managers, designers, and engineers to implement new features and improve user experiences.
Participate in team meetings, technical discussions, and learning opportunities to build strong full-stack engineering skills.
Experience
Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related STEM field.
Familiarity with at least one programming language such as Python, Golang, JavaScript, or TypeScript.
Exposure to web development concepts and frontend frameworks such as React, Next.js, or similar technologies.
Basic understanding of REST APIs, databases, and version control (Git).
Interest in learning about cloud platforms, microservices architecture, and modern DevOps practices.
Curiosity about AI/LLMs and their application in real-world software systems is a plus.
Strong problem-solving skills, eagerness to learn, and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
