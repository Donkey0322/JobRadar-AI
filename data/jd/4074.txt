Description
We are seeking a Software Engineer Intern to join our Full Stack Development team. In this role, you will contribute to the design, development, and delivery of web applications and services that support Sargent & Lundy’s internal operations and client-facing solutions. This hands-on internship blends back-end and front-end development with scripting, automation, and cross-functional collaboration — providing real-world experience across the full software development lifecycle. Key Responsibilities include: Application DevelopmentDevelop and maintain RESTful APIs using C# .NET, following established architectural patterns and coding standards.Build and enhance interactive, user-facing web applications using modern frameworks and libraries such as React, MVC, and related technologies.Develop ad-hoc scripts and utilities to support business operations and streamline internal workflows using Python, Go, PowerShell, or other appropriate languages.Collaboration & DeliveryCollaborate with fellow developers, IT professionals, and end users to design, build, and integrate both homegrown and commercial applications.Coordinate internal customer requirements, architect programmatic solutions, execute thorough testing, and produce clear documentation of completed work.Innovation & Continuous ImprovementContinuously learn and present new techniques at Communities of Practice sessions to IT staff.Evaluate and recommend open-source tools, libraries, and other add-ins that can accelerate development timelines and improve code quality.
Qualifications
We do not sponsor employees for work authorization in the U.S. for this position. Required Qualifications:Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or a related technical field.Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking skills with a desire to tackle complex technical challenges.Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively across technical and non-technical audiences.Experience or academic coursework in the following:C# / .NETReactHTML, CSS, JavaScript, JSONMVC architectureSOAP and REST APIsSQL ServerVisual StudioMicrosoft Azure (Portal, DevOps)GitHubDesired Qualifications:Experience with Active Directory concepts including authentication, authorization, and identity management.Familiarity with Power BI for data visualization and business intelligence reporting.Exposure to ASP.NET Core Web APIs and modern, cross-platform API development.Knowledge of Azure Functions and API Management (APIM).Understanding of microservices architecture and distributed system design.Passion for continuous learning and advancing best practices in software engineering.Ability to work in a fast-paced, multidisciplinary environment with high visibility and accountability.
