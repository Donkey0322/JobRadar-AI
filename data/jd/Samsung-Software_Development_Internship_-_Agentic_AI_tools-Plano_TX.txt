Role and Responsibilities About the Team: The Network Tool Automation Team is a dynamic and innovative group at the forefront of developing cutting-edge solutions to streamline the commissioning and auditing processes for Samsung network products. By leveraging advanced automation technologies, the team enhances operational efficiency, minimizes manual intervention, and ensures seamless integration of network devices. The team's work is pivotal in maintaining the reliability and performance of Samsung's network infrastructure, which is critical to the company's success in the telecommunications industry. Key Focus: A key focus of the team is the development of an Agentic AI System, a groundbreaking initiative designed to assist in troubleshooting network issues on a day-to-day basis. This innovative system employs artificial intelligence to analyze network data, identify anomalies, and provide actionable insights, significantly reducing downtime and improving overall network performance. The Agentic AI System is a testament to the team's commitment to leveraging the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and data analytics to deliver robust and scalable solutions. Responsibilities: Design and Implement Automated Scripts: Develop and optimize automated scripts to enhance existing tools and processes, ensuring they are efficient, reliable, and scalable. Collaborate with Cross-Functional Teams: Work closely with various departments to address complex network challenges, ensuring that solutions are comprehensive and effective. Utilize Advanced Technologies: Leverage the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and data analytics to continuously improve the team's solutions, ensuring they meet the evolving needs of Samsung's network infrastructure. Contribute to the Agentic AI System: Play a key role in the development of the Agentic AI System, gaining hands-on experience with state-of-the-art technologies and contributing to its evolution. Work on Transformative Projects: Engage in projects that are at the cutting edge of technology, helping to evolve Samsung's network infrastructure and make a substantial impact in the telecommunications industry. Qualifications: Education: Currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree. Preferred academic fields of study: Computer Science, Data Engineering, Business Analytics, or any related field. Rising Seniors or Recent Grads strongly preferred. Technical Skills: Proficiency in Python, React, Django, FastAPI, SQL/NoSQL databases, Unix OS, and Bash scripting. Experience building Agentic AI solutions. Skills: Strong communication and leadership abilities. Effective multitasking skills. Onsite collaboration experience. Opportunities for Growth: This role offers an exciting opportunity to work on transformative projects that are shaping the future of Samsung's network infrastructure. Interns will have the chance to collaborate with experienced professionals, gaining valuable insights and hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies. The internship provides a platform for personal and professional growth, allowing interns to make a substantial impact in the telecommunications industry while contributing to the evolution of Samsung's network solutions. Conclusion: Join the Network Tool Automation Team as a Software Development Intern and be part of a team that is revolutionizing the way Samsung manages its network infrastructure.
