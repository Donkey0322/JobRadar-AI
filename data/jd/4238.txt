Our Summer Internship Program is designed to provide meaningful, hands-on experience where you will work on real projects, solve real business challenges, and collaborate with leaders across the organization.
This is more than an internship—it’s an opportunity to accelerate your development, expand your perspective, and begin shaping your career with purpose.
We are looking for curious, driven, and high-potential individuals ready to learn, contribute, and grow. If you are eager to make an impact, challenge yourself, and be part of a team that values innovation and continuous improvement, we invite you to apply and take the first step toward your future with us.
About the Role
Dover Fueling Solutions, ClearView, is looking for a intuitive and motivated Summer Intern to join ClearView team to modernize the legacy soul with new technology This role offers an exciting opportunity to work on real-world fueling systems while gaining hands-on experience in software quality engineering.
The role with get to understand the applications and the business logic behind creating the day close of inventory and sales at the end of day happening at the gas stations.
Key Responsibilities
Convert the legacy VB6 applications to modern technology c#.
Directly work with senior developers to gain the domain knowledge.
After finishing the applications add the testing scenarios to test the application.
Preferred Qualifications
A proactive attitude with a strong desire to learn.
To do attitude and to finish the work on time.
Should be fast learner and responsible.
Ability to code in .Net environment.
Basin knowledge of c# programming
What You’ll Gain
Basic knowledge of azure dev-ops and git hub and branching strategies.
.Net, C# and queueing mechanisms
Mentorship from experienced developers.
The opportunity to work with modern tools, frameworks, and engineering practices.

"socialSrc": '',

});

if (j2w.Args.get('isResponsive')) {
$('.frequency-error-message').html(result.concat('<br/>'));

} else {

}
}
