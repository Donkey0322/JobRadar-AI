Job Description: Primary Function of Position&nbsp;
The intern will help make key enhancements to a rapidly growing and recently commercialized product. The product spans mobile app, web, cloud, desktop software, ML algorithms and hospital installed software. Primary focus of intern will be to prototype 3D visualization algorithms to display anatomic structures for surgical planning purpose. The intern will be supervised by senior engineers.Essential Job Duties&nbsp;* Develop 3D visualization feature for mobile and/or web apps to display anatomic structures
* Develop interactive 3D image processing algorithms for mobile or web apps
