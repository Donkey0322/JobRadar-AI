Job Description:
Engineering Integration Associate Internship
Company Overview
At Mitsubishi Power, we're not just building better clean energy technologies; we're architecting a better future. Our team is boldly redefining power generation to accelerate the world's energy transition. We operate as one team, pushing toward our vision of the future. We value problem solvers, prioritize collaboration, and support each other in an inclusive culture built on accountability and authenticity by demonstrating our values: Safety, Family, Innovative, Inclusive, Accountable & Courageous. Together, we’re building the future we all aspire to - making net zero a reality.
Role Overview
The internship on the Mitsubishi Power Engineering Integration team allows a current student to gain valuable experience in the Power Industry. During their time with Mitsubishi, interns will perform an array of tasks that have direct impact on current internal and external projects we are executing. Mitsubishi will give the intern terrific experience in real world systems integration and product lifecycle management.
Key Responsibilities
Demonstrates our core competencies: Action oriented, change champion, customer-focused, developing self & others, and ownership
Collaborate with the collective Engineering teams to support the development and deployment of new software tools for product configuration and requirements management.
Contribute to the development and refinement of project requirements by collaborating with stakeholders, ensuring clarity, completeness, and feasibility.
Contribute to the development and refinement of product configuration models and ensuring software development meets the requirements agreed with stakeholders.
Assist the Group Managers, Execution and Applications engineering with short-term and long-term work assignments and achieve the required results in a timely fashion and with high quality. Tasks may include schedule development & template creation, process improvement, database support, integration testing, and other common duties.
Gain a basic understanding of Power Industry technology and how products are scoped and executed.
Requirements
Active student currently obtaining a bachelor’s degree (BS) in Engineering from a four-year College or University.
The Ideal candidate should have an understanding of Requirements Management, Configuration Control, and the V-Model of systems development.
Moderate knowledge in MS Office applications (Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel, Word, etc.).
Basic understanding of a PLM and/or Project Management tool (Windchill, Codebeamer, Teamcenter, DevOps, Jira, and Jama for example) is a plus.
Why Should You Apply?
Excellent Benefits (Medical, Dental, Vision & 401K Matching).
Excellent development programs and advancement opportunities.
Tuition reimbursement and on-the-job training.
Paid vacation, sick time, and holidays.
Committed to quality products and services.
Great working environment and culture.
Employee Appreciation Programs and Event.
