Breeze Airways is transforming how people travel with a focus on simplicity, kindness, and innovation. As an Intern, Software Engineer, you'll work alongside our engineering team to build the software systems that power our operations, from booking and reservations to in-flight experiences and operations management. You'll contribute to meaningful projects that directly impact thousands of guests flying Breeze every day.
Here's what you'll do
Scope of Impact: Individual contributions within a single team
Build and Ship Features:
Collaborate with engineers, product managers, and designers to develop software solutions that enhance the Breeze customer and operational experience
Write clean, maintainable code for front-end and back-end systems using our tech stack (Ruby on Rails, Angular, Go, PostgreSQL)
Contribute to feature development across our web applications, mobile apps, and internal tools
Grow your technical skillset and receive mentorship from your team members.
Ensure Quality:
Write thorough unit tests and participate in code reviews
Debug and resolve issues in development and production environments
Learn and Grow:
Work under the guidance of other engineers to understand complex systems and best practices
Continuously expand your technical skills as our platform and technology evolves
Drive Impact:
Contribute ideas to improve customer experiences and operational efficiency
Participate in technical discussions and planning sessions
Help maintain documentation and share knowledge with the team
Here's what you'll need to be successful
Minimum Qualifications
0-2 years of professional software development experience
Developing foundation in computer science fundamentals: data structures, algorithms, and system design
Understanding of Git version control and collaborative development workflows
Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Active enrollment in a 4-year degree in Computer Science or related field
Skills/Talents
Self-starter with a positive attitude and strong desire for success
High performance orientation with ability to work well under pressure
Strong attention to detail, organization, and time management
Ability to complete projects with guidance and meet deadlines
Exemplifies Breeze's values of Safety, Kindness, Integrity, Ingenuity, and Excellence
