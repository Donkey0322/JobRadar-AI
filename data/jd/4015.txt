The Digital Support Summer Associate will support the Business Intelligence team in Operational Excellence. The position is responsible for supporting the development of catalogs, reports, and maintaining documentation. The associate will contribute to the development and enhancement of solutions with the Power Platform with a focus on PowerBI reporting. Provide hands-on assistance with internal application development projects across various platforms.
Key Responsibilities
Development of PowerBI reporting
Contribute to UI design, writing and debugging code, and integrating third-party services
Working with a range of programming languages and technologies, such as M-Query, JSON, SQL, and Python, depending on the project requirements.
Collaborate with experienced cross-disciplinary Mitsubishi personnel to conceive, design, and develop innovative products and services
Support creation of all documentation related to development and use of applications
Ability to manage product/application development with minimal guidance
Meet project deadlines as indicated in a project plan and assigned tasks
Able to estimate work for defined features
Support implementing new features on existing products, fixing bugs, and improving performance
Commit code with detailed notes working with source control mechanisms
Perform other such duties as may be required
Comply with all company policies and procedures.

Programming experience with at least one modern server language
Computer Science fundamentals in object-oriented design, data structures and algorithm design, and complexity analysis
Exposure to all stages of development: design, development, testing, and deployment
