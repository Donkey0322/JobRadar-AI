Overview
LMI is seeking a Full Stack Development Intern to support continued build out of the SupplierDB for the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA). The intern will: assist with research and development on LLM-powered inference; help build and optimize search and scraping pipelines; learn about deployment to Kubernetes environments; and generally be part of a development team building agentic workflows that collate, curate, and surface data from internet-backed sources. LMI is a new breed of digital solutions provider dedicated to accelerating government impact with innovation and speed. Investing in technology and prototypes ahead of need, LMI brings commercial-grade platforms and mission-ready AI to federal agencies at commercial speed. Leveraging our mission-ready technology and solutions, proven expertise in federal deployment, and strategic relationships, we enhance outcomes for the government, efficiently and effectively. With a focus on agility and collaboration, LMI serves the defense, space, healthcare, and energy sectors—helping agencies navigate complexity and outpace change. Headquartered in Tysons, Virginia, LMI is committed to delivering impactful results that strengthen missions and drive lasting value.
Responsibilities
Success in this role comes from strong communication skills, organization, adaptability, and an interest in full stack development. Day-to-day responsibilities may include:Independent coding reviews.Feature build-outs in concert with other teammates.Building out unit- and integration-tests.Conducting research on scraping efficacy at scale.Conducting research on text-to-prediction inferences.Attending team meetings and coordinating development efforts with teammates.
Qualifications
Minimum QualificationsCurrently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in computer science, data science, machine learning, or a related fieldStrong proficiency in Python and SQLCurrent enrollment in a degree seeking program required Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skillsAbility to work effectively both independently and in team environmentsDesired Skills & BackgroundInterest in manufacturing and supply chains.Background in developing LLM-powered workflows.Applicants must meet eligibility requirements for a U.S. Government security clearance. Only US Citizens are eligible for a security clearance. For this position, LMI will only consider applicants with security clearances or applicants who are eligible for security clearances, due to the nature of the work.
