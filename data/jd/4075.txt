A Career with Point72's L/S Equities Team
Long/Short Equity is Point72’s core strategy and its success is dependent upon our sector-based investing teams. Using fundamental research, our research analysts inform the investment strategies of our portfolio managers. Through our Point72 University, you have access to an unparalleled training and coaching curriculum to help foster your success. We offer you a clear path based on your abilities, willingness to work hard, and performance. Join us if you're looking for a career at the forefront of investing.
What you’ll do
Gain exposure to researching generative AI and code-assistant tools to for potential equity research workflows within the L/S Equities team in our New York office
Support prototyping and iterating on AI-driven tools that surface investment insights and automate manual tasks
Assist with implementing AI models and integrating coding-focused tools into developer workflows
Evaluate model outputs, run basic experiments, and measure solution impact
Collaborate with investment professionals and internal data teams to understand data needs, access sources, and translate requirements into technical tasks
Help identify repetitive processes and scope opportunities for automation within research and analyst workflows
Document prototypes, maintain reproducible code, and present findings to the team
Summer Internship Program at Point72
In addition to the learning and development you'll receive in your day-to-day role with your team, as a part of our summer internship program you will:
Work alongside your fellow interns and be mentored by experienced professionals
Meet your peers through designated intern programming, volunteer opportunities, and social events
Attend sessions with senior leaders discussing their expertise and career paths
Get hands-on access to best-in-class tools and resources
Gain exposure to groups and functions with unique responsibilities and perspectives on our industry
Develop technical, writing, and presentation skills
What's Required
Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a closely related field
Hands-on programming experience in Python and familiarity with SQL or equivalent data query languages
Practical experience implementing or integrating AI models or AI APIs into applications
Demonstrated experience using coding-focused developer tools and AI code assistants, explicitly including Cursor and Codex
Ability to identify manual or repetitive workflows and propose concrete automation opportunities
Experience collaborating with data teams or data engineers to access, validate, and instrument datasets
Strong problem-solving skills, curiosity about AI tools, and eagerness to learn in a fast-paced team environment
Commitment to the highest ethical standards
About Point72
Point72 is a leading global alternative investment firm led by Steven A. Cohen. Building on more than 30 years of investing experience, Point72 seeks to deliver superior returns for its investors through fundamental and systematic investing strategies across asset classes and geographies. We aim to attract and retain the industry's brightest talent by cultivating an investor-led culture and committing to our people's long-term growth. For more information, visit https://point72.com/1.
