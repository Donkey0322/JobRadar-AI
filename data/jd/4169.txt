About Cartesian
Cartesian is building spatial intelligence for indoor environments to drive operational efficiency. We’re tackling one of the biggest challenges in the $35T global retail industry: in-store inventory visibility. Our platform delivers accurate indoor positioning and actionable product location insights, helping retailers streamline operations, optimize workflows, and reduce inefficiencies. By fusing wireless signals and mobile computer vision, we provide a uniquely scalable, infrastructure-free solution already deployed by international fashion brands.
Founded by an MIT engineering professor and alumni behind award-winning, patented core technologies, Cartesian spun out in 2023. Originally backed by a U.S. National Science Foundation SBIR Award, we’ve bootstrapped to a live product now deployed in multiple countries and are scaling aggressively.

What We Work On
We're scaling a platform that processes billions of wireless signal readings to locate products inside retail stores across multiple countries. Some of the problems our engineering team works on:
Indoor positioning at scale: Fusing noisy RFID and computer vision data into accurate, real-time product locations across hundreds of stores
Data infrastructure: Building efficient pipelines to ingest, process, and serve TB-scale datasets with tight latency and cost constraints
ML in production: Training, deploying, and monitoring ML models that run on mobile devices and cloud infrastructure.
Mobile and edge: Design mobile (Android) SDKs that integrate with enterprise PDAs and wireless scanners.
Platform reliability: Scaling Kubernetes-based infrastructure on Azure to support rapid international deployment.
Product development: Shipping dashboards, APIs, and tools that help retail operations teams make better decisions every day
