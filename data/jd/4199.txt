Description
Arrow International is the world's largest manufacturer and supplier of charitable gaming products and solutions. We produce and distribute a wide array of products including consumables (pull tab tickets, bingo paper and ink, etc.) as well as world class, state-of-the-art, electronic gaming products. Our products are sold, installed, and operated in numerous social and gaming venues around the world. We continue to grow at a record pace and offer this exciting career opportunity to join our team where we are focused on attracting and engaging exceptional talent, empowering colleagues to achieve fulfilling careers, and creating fun and engaging products that are second to none for our customers.Position SummaryArrow International, Inc. is seeking to add a Software Engineer intern (Paid) to our Electronic Gaming Development team. This position is integral to the development of and successful launch of new and exciting electronic gaming products, as part of our newly formed Electronic Gaming Division. As an Intern you will work with Unity and C# to develop cutting edge 3D Games. Primary responsibilities will be to create a game by combining ideas, concepts, art, and assets with existing and new code into a functional and engaging game. This is a Summer Internship slated to start in the middle of May. Primary Roles and ResponsibilitiesExcellent understanding of computer science fundamentals (OOP, data structures, algorithms) Practical experience in C#.General understanding of common development practices (version control, agile development) Ability to quickly understand, debug, and modify existing code Strong analytical technical problem-solving skills.Applicants must be able to work at our studio in Las Vegas, NV.
Requirements
Experience and EducationDegree or working towards a degree in computer science, computer engineering, or related field.Strong understanding of object-oriented programming principles in C# required.Unity experience preferred.Experience in Casino or Slot Related Industries is nice to have.
