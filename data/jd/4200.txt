Role Overview
As an AI Engineering Intern, you’ll work at the intersection of software development, large language models, predictive analytics, and investment analysis. You’ll tackle real problems on production systems—not toy projects—and gain hands-on experience building AI/ML solutions that transform how we identify and evaluate investment opportunities in biomedical and climate technologies.
Key Responsibilities
Assist in designing and developing end-to-end AI applications, from prototype through deployment
Build and iterate on AI agents, workflows, and tool integrations (e.g., MCP servers, agentic pipelines)
Contribute to production-grade forecasting or classification systems with attention to latency, scalability, and robustness
Help develop full-stack AI applications with both frontend and backend components
Work with proprietary research data to train, evaluate, and improve ML or GenAI models
Participate in code reviews, design discussions, and cross-functional collaboration with analysts and investors
