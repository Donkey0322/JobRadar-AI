Requisition Summary
A Summer Internship at PACCAR Financial provides the opportunity to learn first-hand from the industry's top professionals and be part of the Application Software development team responsible for leasing applications. The intern will be integrated into the team and can make a meaningful contribution to our dynamic organization, while enhancing your resume. PACCAR interns have a great deal of visibility within the company. Don’t miss out on an exciting opportunity!
The position will be in Bellevue, WA.Job Functions / Responsibilities
Testing and Debugging: Test applications thoroughly, including AI features, to identify and resolve bugs or performance issues.
Documentation: Maintain technical documentation, including AI model descriptions and usage guidelines.
Learning and Development: Continuously upgrade skills related to AI frameworks, tools, and algorithms.
Maintenance and Support: Help troubleshoot issues in AI-enabled applications and assist in updating models as needed.
Participate in our Agile practices, including daily Scrum meetings, Sprint Planning, and Retrospectives
Participate in job and department specific duties and processes as assigned
Identify, research, and resolve complex technical problems related to internal applications and software
Qualifications
Knowledge of AI methodologies such as GitHub Copilot, RAG etc.
Self-motivation and energetic personality.
Excellent written and oral communication skills.
Solid understanding of Object-Oriented programming and software design methodologies.
Ability to develop applications in C#, .NET (including ASP.NET), HTML5, Java-Script, and SQL.
Ability to work effectively in a team environment, customer support environment, and with all levels of the organization.
Education
Bachelor's Degree in Progress pursuing one of the following degrees: Information Systems,Computer Science, or other related programs.
Junior or senior standing preferred
Company Information
PACCAR is a Fortune 500 company established in 1905. PACCAR Inc is recognized as a global leader in the commercial vehicle, financial, and customer service fields with internationally recognized brands such as Kenworth, Peterbilt, and DAF trucks. PACCAR is a global technology leader in the design, manufacture and customer support of premium light-, medium- and heavy-duty trucks under the Kenworth, Peterbilt and DAF nameplates and also provides customized financial services, information technology and truck parts related to its principal business.
