Job Summary:
Belden is seeking a Software Engineering Co-Op to work alongside the Precision OT Software Engineering team in designing, developing, and maintaining production, test, and end-user software packages. In this role, you will gain hands-on experience by following the software development lifecycle, ensuring the delivery of high-quality software solutions that meet customer needs.
This role reports to Software Engineering Technical Director and is onsite in Rochester, NY.
You will make a difference in the following ways:
Assist in designing, developing, and maintaining software solutions for production, testing, and end-user applications.
Follow the software development life cycle to ensure high-quality software delivery.
Work with the team to research and integrate external software packages.
Gain hands-on experience with networking concepts and database management.
Contribute to the development of REST APIs and web applications.
What you bring:
Currently pursuing a Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, or Computer Science degree, with at least two years completed (one year for graduate students).
Basic knowledge of software development techniques and tools.
Experience with both a compiled language and a scripting language (Python preferred).
Familiarity with networking concepts, including IP addressing, sockets, and routing.
Exposure to relational databases (MySQL, SQLite, or similar).
Willingness to research and integrate external software packages.
Experience with web application development and JavaScript frameworks (Node, Angular) is a plus.
Exposure to time-series databases and SNMP is a plus.
