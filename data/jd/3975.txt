About the Identity Team:
The Identity team owns the core services that power how members and workforce users securely access Transcarent experiences. This includes:
Member Identity Services – login, registration, account recovery, and management of member identifiers across systems (user IDs, person IDs, membership IDs, etc.).
Workforce Identity Services – corporate identity and accounts for employees and partners using platforms like Okta and Ping
SSO and Federation – inbound and outbound SSO for member apps and integrations with customers and partners.
The team builds and operates secure, highly available backend services and APIs running in modern cloud infrastructure, integrating with Okta, Ping Identity, and related tooling.
