About the Role
We are seeking a highly motivated Software Engineer Intern in Operation Platforms to join our Operation Platforms team and contribute to the systems that power Bot Auto’s autonomous fleet operations. Our Operation Platforms group builds the intelligence and automation behind fleet scheduling, fleet management, fleet maintenance, mission control, remote assistance, IoT platform, business intelligence, and operational efficiency. As an intern, you will work hands-on with experienced engineers to design, develop, and ship features within one or more of these domains—directly impacting how our autonomous trucks are dispatched, monitored, maintained, and optimized in real-world commercial freight operations.
What You'll Do
Contribute to the development of backend services and/or frontend tools for fleet operations platforms, including scheduling, mission control, and fleet monitoring systems
Build automation workflows that improve operational efficiency, reduce manual intervention, and enhance system reliability
Work on automated pipelines and services that process real-time and historical fleet data for analytics and decision-making
Develop features for IoT platforms that integrate vehicle telemetry, sensors, and remote assistance systems
Collaborate with engineers and product teams to design scalable and user-friendly tools for operations teams
Participate in debugging, testing, and improving system performance and reliability in production environments
Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code following engineering best practices
