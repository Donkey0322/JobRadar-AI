About EarnIn
As one of the first pioneers of earned wage access, our passion at EarnIn is building products that deliver real-time financial flexibility for those with the unique needs of living paycheck to paycheck. Our community members access their earnings as they earn them, with options to spend, save, and grow their money without mandatory fees, interest rates, or credit checks.
We’re fortunate to have an incredibly experienced leadership team, combined with world-class funding partners like A16Z, Matrix Partners, DST, Ribbit Capital, and a very healthy core business with a tremendous runway. We’re growing fast and are excited to continue bringing world-class talent onboard to help shape the next chapter of our growth journey.POSITION SUMMARY
We are seeking a motivated iOS Engineering Intern to join our 12-week internship program. In this role, you will work on real-world mobile features that directly impact our community members and contribute to production iOS applications. You will collaborate closely with iOS, backend, product, and design teams to build intuitive, performant, and reliable mobile experiences. This is an opportunity to gain hands-on experience developing user-facing features, working with modern iOS technologies, and shipping code in a fast-paced fintech environment.
This role requires hybrid work from our Mountain View office, with 2 days per week in person. The internship pays $30/hour, with an expected 40 hours per week over the 12-week program.
WHAT YOU'LL DO
Contribute to the development and enhancement of features in EarnIn’s iOS application and web surfaces
Collaborate with backend engineers to integrate APIs and deliver seamless end-to-end user experiences
Write clean, maintainable, and efficient Swift and React code following engineering best practices
Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality releases
Work closely with product and design teams to translate requirements into intuitive mobile experiences
Gain exposure to modern iOS and web development paradigms and tools
Learn and apply best practices for performance, reliability, and usability in mobile applications
WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
How to build and ship production-quality features in a large-scale iOS and web application
Best practices for writing clean, maintainable, and performant mobile code
Fundamentals of mobile and web architecture and modern development paradigms
How to collaborate with backend, product, and design teams to deliver end-to-end features
Debugging, testing, and improving client-side performance and reliability
Working in a fast-paced, product-driven environment with real user impact
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related technical field
Strong foundation in computer science fundamentals, including data structures, algorithms, and debugging
Proficiency in Swift with an interest in iOS development
Curiosity to learn or experience in front end web development(e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, React)
Familiarity with mobile development concepts or iOS frameworks (e.g., UIKit or SwiftUI) is a plus
Curiosity and willingness to learn
Ability to communicate ideas clearly and collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Excited to build user-facing products and improve the mobile experience for real users
Prior internship or project experience is a plus, but not required
