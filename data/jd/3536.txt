About League
Founded in 2014, League is the leading healthcare consumer experience (CX) platform, powered by artificial intelligence (AI), reaching more than 63 million people around the world and delivering the highest level of personalization in the industry. Payers, providers, and consumer health partners build on League’s platform to deliver high-engagement healthcare solutions proven to improve health outcomes. League has raised over $285 million in venture capital funding to date, powering the digital experiences for some of healthcare’s most trusted brands, including Highmark Health, Manulife, Medibank, and Shoppers Drug Mart.
A&S Backend Engineer
Join our Apps & Solutions Backend Engineering team and help implement the League platform for our customers. As a Backend Engineer Co-op, you’ll gain hands-on experience designing and building production backend services, APIs, and data systems that support real users at scale.
We are an AI-forward engineering organization. We leverage modern AI tools extensively across our development lifecycle, from design and prototyping to coding, testing, documentation, and refactoring. We’re looking for candidates who are not only strong engineers but who actively use AI tools to accelerate learning, improve code quality, and increase impact.
This co-op is your opportunity to sharpen your backend fundamentals while working in a fast-moving, AI-enabled environment where engineering velocity and quality both matter.
What You’ll Work On
Designing and building REST and WebSocket APIs
Developing scalable backend services in Go
Building new products and integrations
Writing clean, testable, maintainable production code
Participating in code reviews and architectural discussions
Improving test automation and system reliability
Leveraging AI tools to accelerate development, debugging, documentation, and testing
Contributing to technical design and implementation planning
What We’re Looking For
Core Engineering Skills
Experience building backend applications (academic, personal, or professional projects)
Strong programming fundamentals (data structures, APIs, system design basics)
Hands-on coding experience in Go or another backend language
Understanding of databases and API design principles
Exposure to automated testing practices
AI Tooling & AI-Enabled Development (Strongly Preferred)
Hands-on experience using AI coding assistants (e.g., ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude, etc.)
Ability to effectively prompt, evaluate, and iterate with AI tools
Demonstrated use of AI to improve productivity, code quality, debugging, or learning
Comfort working in an AI-augmented engineering workflow
Curiosity about how AI can improve engineering systems and processes
We strongly prefer candidates who already integrate AI tools into their daily development workflow and can articulate how those tools improve their output.
Nice to Have

Exposure to cloud platforms (GCP preferred)
Experience contributing to team projects
