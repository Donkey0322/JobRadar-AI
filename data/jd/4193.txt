About EarnIn
As one of the first pioneers of earned wage access, our passion at EarnIn is building products that deliver real-time financial flexibility for those with the unique needs of living paycheck to paycheck. Our community members access their earnings as they earn them, with options to spend, save, and grow their money without mandatory fees, interest rates, or credit checks.
We’re fortunate to have an incredibly experienced leadership team, combined with world-class funding partners like A16Z, Matrix Partners, DST, Ribbit Capital, and a very healthy core business with a tremendous runway. We’re growing fast and are excited to continue bringing world-class talent onboard to help shape the next chapter of our growth journey.
POSITION SUMMARY
We are seeking a motivated Backend Engineering Intern to join our 12-week internship program. In this role, you will work on real-world backend systems, contribute to production services, and collaborate closely with engineers, product managers, and cross-functional partners. The Offers team is a fast-growing, high-impact area at EarnIn, focused on building systems that deliver personalized, scalable value to our community members. This is an opportunity to gain hands-on experience building backend services, working with modern microservice architectures, and solving problems at scale in a fast-paced fintech environment.
This role requires hybrid work from our Mountain View office, with 2 days per week in person. The internship pays $30/hour, with an expected 40 hours per week over the 12-week program.
WHAT YOU'LL DO
Contribute to the design, development, and maintenance of backend services for the Offers platform
Build APIs and services that power user-facing features and partner integrations
Collaborate with product, design, data science, and analytics teams to deliver end-to-end solutions
Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code following engineering best practices
Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality software
Gain exposure to distributed systems, microservices architecture, and cloud infrastructure
Learn and apply industry-standard tools and technologies used in large-scale systems
WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
How to build backend systems that power real-world financial products at scale
Designing and developing APIs and services with production-quality standards
Fundamentals of scalable system design and microservice architecture
Writing clean, maintainable, and efficient code in a collaborative codebase
Working cross-functionally with product, design, and data partners to ship features end-to-end
Debugging, testing, and improving system reliability in a live production environment
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related technical field
Strong foundation in computer science fundamentals, including data structures, algorithms, and debugging
Proficiency in at least one programming language such as Java, Kotlin, Python, Go, or similar
Familiarity with backend development concepts such as APIs and databases
Exposure to relational or NoSQL databases (e.g., Postgres, MySQL, DynamoDB) is a plus
Demonstrated curiosity and willingness to learn
Ability to make thoughtful decisions and clearly communicate tradeoffs and ideas
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Prior internship experience is a plus, but not required
