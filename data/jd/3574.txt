As an intern for the summer of 2026, you will join one of our backend engineering teams (listed below) and contribute to the development of our medi buying and measurement platform. In this role you will design, build, and improve product features that directly impact our customers. This internship offers the opportunity to take ownership of a meaningful project, work with real-world data, and gain hands-on experience building scalable systems in a fast-paced technology environment.
Campaign Management: The Campaign Manager team builds and owns Tatari’s media buying applications and pipelines. The team focuses on delivering campaign planning applications and features that allows media buyers – both internal and external – to plan, execute and optimize TV campaigns using powerful intuitive tools provided by Tatari’s data and ML models, and drive self-service platforms.
Media Intelligence: The Media Intelligence team builds the AI/ML systems that power automated media planning, audience targeting, and prediction models for clearance and performance across linear and streaming television. Our focus is on intelligent automation, predictive insights, and AI-powered tools that enable optimal, data-driven campaign decisions. Our work is at the intersection of AI/ML engineering, data pipelines, and product to make media intelligence available to every Tatari client.
Reporting and Measurement: The Reporting and Measurement pillar consists of 2 different verticals that focus on building the products and systems that help clients understand how their TV campaigns are performing. Intuitive Reporting (IR) owns the client-facing measurement and reporting platform, including the dashboards, APIs, and analytics that turn TV advertising data into clear, actionable insights for brands and agencies. RAM Data Quality (RDQ) owns the integrity of the data behind every report and dashboard at Tatari, building the systems that keep our measurement data accurate, complete, and trustworthy at scale. It's the foundation that everything else, including our AI-powered products, depends on.
